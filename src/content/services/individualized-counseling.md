Weekly one-on-one work with a licensed clinician. Sessions run 45 minutes
and draw from three evidence-based methods:

- **Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)** — understand how thoughts, feelings,
  and actions connect, and practice changing the patterns that keep you stuck.
- **Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)** — build skills for distress
  tolerance, emotion regulation, and stronger relationships.
- **Solution-focused** — set concrete, near-term goals and work backward
  from the life you want.

You can attend counseling on its own, or combine it with our **Level 1
program** (one individual session plus a 1.5-hour group meeting each week).
We'll help you figure out the right fit on your first call.
