Two complementary practices offered as standalone sessions or alongside
your other care.

**Mindfulness meditation** focuses on breathing, guided imagery, and
noticing what's here right now — without judgment. Beginners are welcome;
you don't need any experience. Sessions are quiet, guided, and paced so
you can settle in.

**Reiki** is a gentle, hands-on form of energy work where the practitioner
serves as a conduit for calming, restorative energy. You'll stay fully
clothed and the touch is light or non-touch, depending on your comfort.
Many people find reiki deeply relaxing on its own, and a useful complement
to talk therapy.

Both practices are open to anyone — they don't require enrollment in
another program.
