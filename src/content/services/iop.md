If you need more support than weekly counseling but don't require inpatient
care, our Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) gives you structure while you
keep living at home, working, and caring for family.

You'll join a small group three evenings a week for topic-focused sessions
on triggers, coping, and recovery. You'll also meet one-on-one with a
counselor each week for 30 minutes. The program is educational and
supportive — not a lecture series — and the people in the room are
working through the same things you are.

Most participants stay in IOP for 8–12 weeks, depending on your progress
and your clinician's recommendation. We can talk through what that looks
like for you when you call.
