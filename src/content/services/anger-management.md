A state-approved program that helps you understand what sets you off, how
to respond without harm, and how to rebuild trust in your relationships.

Sessions blend skill-building with reiki, mindfulness, and meditation so
you leave each meeting with something practical and something calming.
The format works whether you're attending voluntarily, fulfilling a court
or probation requirement, or referring yourself because something needs
to change.

We can coordinate with your attorney, probation officer, or referring
provider as needed. Ask us about that when you call.
